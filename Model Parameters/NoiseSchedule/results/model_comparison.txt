Key observations:
- Quadratic schedule achieves the best mean RMSE (0.1147) and lowest variance (Std=0.0326),
  indicating the most consistent and accurate reconstructions
- Sigmoid is a close second (0.1357) with similar variance to cosine
- Cosine is mid-tier (0.1692); competitive on most samples but occasionally high error
- Linear performs worst overall (0.2397) with very high variance (Std=0.1766), driven by
  two outlier runs (result_06: 0.5267, result_10: 0.6395) suggesting poor generalisation
  on harder samples under a linear schedule
- The non-linear schedules (cosine, quadratic, sigmoid) all significantly outperform linear,
  consistent with the literature on improved noise schedule design for diffusion models
