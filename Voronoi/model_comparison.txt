Key observations:
- Model A excels on its native input (scattered) but degrades badly on walk paths
- Model B (epoch 93) outperforms Model A on walk path inputs (0.0488 vs ~0.089)
- Model B generalises less well to scattered sensors (0.0683 vs 0.018) — expected
  due to distribution mismatch; walk paths produce spatially clustered Voronoi inputs
  that are very different from uniform scattered coverage
